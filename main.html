Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generatios and never fails to delight.

Ingradients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 190 celuis
  2. In a bowl, mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
